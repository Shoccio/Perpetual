The University of Perpetual Help System Laguna (UPHSL) has achieved top rankings in AppliedHE's ASEAN University Ranking

In the recent conferment by Applied Higher Education (AppliedHE), the University of Perpetual Help System Laguna ended in Rank 9 among Philippine Private Universities like the University of Sto. Tomas, Ateneo de Manila University, Manila Central University, De La Salle University, Trinity University of Asia, and other top Universities in the Philippines and overall 48th place in AppliedHE Public and Private University Ranking: ASEAN.


The ranking system is known for its methodology using profound aspects of institutional performance. This is apparent across multiple criteria and focuses on teaching, learning, research, employability, community engagement, internationalization, etc. UPHSL excelled in providing a high-quality and versatile education. Employability is published as a separate sub-ranking, UPHSL's dedication to producing highly employable graduates is reflected in the overall ranking. The university's global recognition as the 9th in the Philippines and 48th in all universities in ASEAN underscores its commitment to shaping future leaders through comprehensive education and enriching research.

The university's success is a motivation to further enhance education quality. As UPHSL remains dedicated to providing a holistic education, this achievement is the product of all hard work, patience, and dedication and is now standing in the ASEAN region. The University of Perpetual Help System Laguna's presence among the top 50 institutions reflects its motto that “Character building is Nation Building”. We are building and excelling both educationally and morally and this is significant in the current time.

As UPHSL continues to inspire and make a lasting impact in the field of higher education. Congratulations to the University of Perpetual Help System Laguna for this achievement!
